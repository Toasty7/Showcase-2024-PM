Josiah Harris

Josiah Harris, a visionary entrepreneur and passionate paleontologist, stands as the proud owner of Jurassic Park, a thrilling dinosaur park that brings the prehistoric world to life. With an academic background in paleontology and a deep-seated fascination for the ancient giants that once roamed the Earth, Josiah embarked on a mission to create an immersive and educational experience for visitors of all ages. His journey began with a childhood love for fossils, which blossomed into a lifelong commitment to sharing the wonders of the past with the present. Under Josiah's leadership, Jurassic Park has become a haven for dinosaur enthusiasts and families seeking adventure. Beyond the lifelike animations and awe-inspiring exhibits, Josiah is dedicated to fostering a sense of wonder and curiosity about Earth's history. His innovative approach to blending entertainment with education has made Jurassic Park a must-visit destination, where the magic of the Mesozoic era comes alive in the 21st century.

Richard Sterling

Meet Richard Sterling, the visionary entrepreneur and proud owner of Jurassic park, a thrilling dinosaur park that transports visitors back in time to the Mesozoic era. With a passion for paleontology and a lifelong fascination with dinosaurs, Richard has turned his childhood dream into a reality. Armed with a degree in paleontology and a keen business acumen, he founded Jurassic park to not only showcase the majestic creatures that once roamed the Earth but also to educate and inspire awe in the hearts of visitors young and old. Richard's commitment to authenticity is evident in the park's meticulously crafted exhibits, featuring lifelike animatronic dinosaurs and immersive environments. His dedication to education extends beyond the park's gates, as Jurassic park actively supports paleontological research and education programs. Richard Sterling is not just a businessman; he's a modern-day explorer, bringing the wonders of prehistoric life to the present day for the enjoyment and enlightenment of all who venture into the captivating world of Jurassic park.
